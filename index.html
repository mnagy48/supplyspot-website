Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Supply Spot Vending?

Supply Spot is an AI-powered micromart vending solution. Think: a smart fridge meets a convenience store. Visitors tap to enter, grab what they want, and walk out — no cashier, no clunky buttons. AI tracks inventory, usage, and purchasing patterns to keep things stocked with what people actually want.

What types of items can be sold in the machines?

A little bit of everything — snacks, drinks (refrigerated or frozen), protein shakes, frozen meals, phone chargers, personal care items, allergy medicine, sunscreen... you name it. We tailor the inventory to your building's needs.

Do I have to maintain or restock the machine?

Nope. Supply Spot is fully managed. We monitor inventory levels, restock as needed, and handle all maintenance. You sit back and let us handle the rest.

Is there any cost to my business or building?

No. Installation is free and we handle all the operational costs. If there's a unique setup or custom branding request, we’ll discuss those up front. But our goal is simple: make your space better without adding more work or cost.

Can the machine be customized to match our branding?

Absolutely. We’ll brand the exterior of the machine with your logo and color scheme so it blends in seamlessly with your space — and elevates it.
